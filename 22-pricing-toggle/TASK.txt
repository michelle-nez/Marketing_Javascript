        Ticket 22: Monthly and Annual Pricing Toggle

        Scenario
        The pricing page needs a toggle between monthly and annual billing that recalculates each plan's displayed price and shows the annual savings.

        Concepts
        checkbox input, math, DOM text updates, localStorage

        Instructions
        1. Open index.html in a browser.
        2. Open script.js in Visual Studio Code or Visual Studio.
        3. Complete each TODO without copying the answer first.
        4. Refresh index.html after every change.
        5. Use the browser console to inspect errors and window.dataLayer.

        Challenge
        Show the total annual price (per-month price times 12) as a small line under each plan when annual billing is selected.

        Definition of done
        - Toggling to annual reduces every plan's displayed price by the discount rate
        - Toggling back to monthly restores the original prices
        - A "Save 20%" badge only shows while annual billing is selected
        - The toggle's last state is remembered after refreshing the page
